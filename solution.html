What We Offer

We help restaurants, individuals, and NGOs connect to reduce waste and help those in need through smart, trackable donations.

For Restaurants

Donate surplus food, reduce waste disposal costs, and get tax benefits. Replate simplifies donations with a few clicks.

For NGOs

Request donations as needed. Get notified when nearby restaurants or individuals post food, books, or clothes.

For Individuals

Share what you no longer need or request help easily. Support your local community and reduce landfill waste.
